现在StatCounter已经出了新版的在公开测试，新版的主要特色功能有：

**(1) Visits/Pageloads Option**

**访问/加载选项**

Easily switch between viewing pageloads and visits on the Projects page.

在项目页面轻易切换加载页面和访问视图。

<img title="pageloads_and_visits" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/pageloads_and_visits.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="170" />

**(2) Switch Projects**

**切换项目**

Quickly flip between projects when viewing your stats – just click the drop-down arrow beside your project name.

在浏览数据时快速翻转项目-只需点击项目名称旁的下拉箭头。

<img title="project_dropdown" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/project_dropdown.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="125" />

**(3) Hourly Stats**

**小时数据**

Watch the hour-by-hour evolution of your stats.

观察每小时的数据变革。

<img title="hourly_stats" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/hourly_stats.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="232" />

**(4) Date Range Selector**

**日期范围选择**

Available for all stats – just click “narrow range”. Choose your preferred date range and check the stats only for that period. Hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly and annual options.

对所有数据有效-只需点击“狭窄范围”。选择你喜欢的日期范围以查看仅在该时间段的数据。可供选项有：每小时，每天，每周，每个月，每个季度，以及每年。

You can also compare different periods if you wish – just click “**Add Comparison Period**”

<img title="date_range_selector" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/date_range_selector.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="259" />

**(5) Keyword Analysis – Options**

**关键词分析-选项**

As for all stats, switch between projects when viewing Keyword Analysis.

查看关键词分析时在项目间切换数据。

Or change the time period being examined by clicking “narrow range”.

或者通过点击“狭窄范围”改变查看的日期范围。

Or download a file of all your keywords.

或者下载所有关键词。

<img title="keyword_analysis" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/keyword_analysis1.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="166" />

**(6) Wrap URLS**

**折叠网址**

Check/uncheck the box to truncate and expand very long URLs.

点选或者不选以折叠或扩展很长的网址。

<img title="wrap_urls" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/wrap_urls.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="168" />

**(7) Search Engines**

**搜索引擎**

View the families of Search Engines sending traffic to your site.

查看给你网站带来流量的搜索引擎种类。

<img title="search_engines" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/search_engines.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="363" />

Click on a Search Engine family to see a breakdown of the regional search engines sending traffic your way. E.g. Google breaks down to google.com, google.co.uk, google.ca etc

点击搜索引擎种类以查看各个区域性搜索引擎给你带来的流量。也就是说，Google细分为google.com，google.co.uk，google.ca等。

<img title="search_engines2" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/search_engines2.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="315" />

**(8) Browsers**

**浏览器**

View the families of Browsers being used by the visitors to your site.

查看访客访问网站使用的浏览器种类。

<img title="browsers" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/browsers.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="263" />

Click on a Browser family to see a breakdown of the different browser versions. E.g. Firefox breaks down to versions 4.0, 3.6, 3.5 etc

点击一个浏览器种类查看不同浏览器版本的细分，即Firefox细分为4.0，3.6，3.5版本等。

<img title="browsers2" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/browsers2.jpg" alt="" width="447" height="330" />

**(9) Simplified Reinstall Process**

**简化的重装程序**

Need to reinstall your StatCounter code? No problem! Simply go to “Config” then “Reinstall Code” – we’ve even created a nifty tool which will confirm if the installation has been successful – just click the button “**Check Installation**“.

需要重装StatCounter代码？没问题！只需去“配置”选择中选择重新“安装代码”-我们已经创建了一个小巧的工具来确认安装是否已经成功-只需点击“检查安装”按钮即可。

<img title="reinstall_code" src="http://blog.statcounter.com/wp-content/plugins/reinstall_code.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="246" />
